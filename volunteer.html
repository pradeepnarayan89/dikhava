Volunteers

NavGurukul was set up to achieve a purpose, a vision. Vision to bring about zero to one transformation in the lives of underprivileged. In an increasingly technology driven world, arming underprivileged with technology is even more relevant. A lot of these kids don’t know English, and hence the phase leg of our curriculum is developed in Hindi. Here are a few tracks for volunteering. To enrol as a volunteer for any track, do any of the tasks and send us an email at volunteer@navgurukul.org. Doing a task ensures that you are serious to volunteer.


Teach our students, remotely

Our students are learning programming, and love to talk to volunteers who have time to share their experiences or knowledge. If you know how to code, it is awesome. If you don’t know how to code, you can still share your other experiences or sensitise our students towards issues you feel close with. To talk to our students just send us an email at talk2help@navgurukul.org, with what you want to talk about and we will connect you with a student. It is best if you can talk on a regular basis (e.g. twice in a week, weekly or monthly) as regular sessions are more helpful.



Building the Curriculum


We are curating and building (wherever necessary) curriculum for following teaching Software Engineering in Hindi teaching English through Group Activities curating other resources like inspiring videos, curriculum to sensitise students towards issues etc. Having taught earlier, helps a lot while building the curriculum. We would advise you to talk to our students and try to teach them for 4-5 hours if you haven’t taught any students previously. If you are interested, send us an email at curriculum@navgurukul.org with a 2 pager curriculum of any topic you feel interested in around technology or learning english or otherwise. The curriculum can be in Hindi or English.


Building Saral, our eLearning platform


We are working on an online platform, called SARAL. More details and demo for the platform is available at saral.navgurukul.org. Our backend is hosted at navgurukul/galileo and frontend is hosted at navgurukul/lennon. While our backend is built using Hapi.js (Node.js), our frontend is built using Angular 2. To get started, solve an existing issue or solve an issue that you have with the platform. If you face any issues while installation, etc. - send us an email and we will solve them at the earliest (usually within 24-48 hours). Send us a forked request, and we will reach back to you for a longer term engagement :)

Let’s Translate


India is perhaps one of the few nations, where our obsession for English has excluded so many people of content that can completely change their lives. We are looking forward for people who can help us translate content from English to Hindi, so that we can make the most critical pieces required to learn programming available to all, irrespective of language. We are going to add support for more languages soon. Please translate any recent news article, and send it to us at translate@navgurukul.org.
