About

NavGurukul is proud to welcome you to its first hackathon at Sarita Vihar. 28 hours of sleepless coding where young and experienced learners alike would come to build something, together. This is the first of the hackathons in the series of monthly hackathons that NavGurukul is planning. Apart from our 10 students, everyone else is welcome to join

The hackathon isn’t a big flamboyant event full of goodies or food, rather we are doing this at our humble 3BHK duplex residential learning center. There will be a furnished kitchen, people to code with, and tons of learning.

If you are keen on braving Delhi’s summers, learn, and network with like-minded learners, this is just what you were looking for. Feel free to get something to eat, and share with everyone else Good way to make friends, eh?
